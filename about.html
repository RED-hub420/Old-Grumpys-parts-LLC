About

Built for contractors, mechanics, and equipment owners

This demo positions Old Grumpy’s Parts as a practical source for used and surplus heavy equipment parts, with a tone that fits the rugged brand instead of sounding like a generic corporate template.

Her husband’s construction background gives the business a believable story: real machines, real take-off parts, real fitment knowledge, and a direct line to buyers who need value more than polished dealership pricing.

What this demo proves

It proves the brand can support a direct parts site, not just a Facebook page or random marketplace posts.

What can be added later

Real product photos, live inventory sync, categories by machine brand, quote requests, and platform checkout integration.

What may still be smarter

Depending on budget and volume, it may still make more sense to use eBay or another established marketplace and let this site work as the branded front end.
