About

Parts sourced with real equipment experience behind them.

Old Grumpy’s Parts is built around used and surplus heavy equipment parts for contractors, mechanics, rebuilders, and owner-operators who want a practical alternative to new-part pricing.

From hydraulic components to undercarriage and electrical parts, the goal is simple: organize inventory clearly, show fitment and condition up front, and make it easy for buyers to reach out when they need help matching a machine.

What we list

Hydraulics, electrical parts, drive motors, undercarriage pieces, and structural components for common machines and jobsite equipment.

How orders work

Customers can browse inventory, review product details, add parts to cart, and submit an order request for follow-up on availability and shipping.

Need a hard-to-find item?

Use the contact page to send a machine model, part number, or description of what you need and we can help track it down.
